## LinkedIn

**A — the library (technical)**
> LLMs can write code, but they can't *render UI* without either a host
> framework or unsafe arbitrary JSX. So I built a third option: a closed,
> semantic JSX vocabulary + a compiler that turns model output into real React,
> safely. ~4× smaller output, prompt-cache friendly, library-agnostic via
> adapters. My portfolio runs on it — the chat generates its own interface.
> Try it (and read the article): https://generative-semantic-ui.vercel.app

> threads I keep pulling: generative UI, narrative DSLs (ArticleLang), and
> honest, intent-first dev tooling (Honest-DD). All open source.
